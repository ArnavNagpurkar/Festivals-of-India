India is a land of festivals and culture. In India, more festivals are celebrated as compared to the whole world.

Some of these festivals welcome the seasons, harvest, rains, and even full moon while the others celebrate religious occasions, like the birthdays of Gods, or advent of local New Years

Most of the festivals in India are celebrated in the whole country but some have different names in the different parts of the country.
